About foildata – foiling maps, spots & downwind foiling runs

foildata is a growing collection of tools and data for the foiling community. We focus on a foiling map of the world, with downwind foiling runs, wing foil spots, parawing spots, wake thief spots, and useful resources for foilers and other water sports.

The goal of foildata is to build a comprehensive, community-driven database of foils, boards, parawings, foiling spots, and downwind routes worldwide — paired with reviews, videos, and practical information.

In addition to spot data, foildata will feature large comparison tables for foils, boards, and parawings, including sorting and filtering tools to help foilers choose the right gear.

foildata is created by project7III, the team behind 7III Water, a water sports tracking app for downwind foiling, parawinging, and other disciplines.

Explore the foiling map to find downwind foiling runs, wing and parawing spots, wake thief spots, and more — and contribute your own spots to help grow the global foiling map.
